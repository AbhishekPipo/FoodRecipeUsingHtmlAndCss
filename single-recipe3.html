Finger Millet Balls

Rinse and soak finger millet in water for 30 minutes. Drain and add the millet to a pot with water and salt. Cook until the millet is soft and the water is absorbed. Serve hot as a porridge or use as a base for dishes like upma or khichdi.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Rinse and soak finger millet in water for 30 minutes.

step 2

Drain and add the millet to a pot with water and salt. Cook until the millet is soft and the water is absorbed.

step 3

Serve hot as a porridge or use as a base for dishes like upma or khichdi.
